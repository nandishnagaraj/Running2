Pranesh - The Mountain Conqueror

Pranesh is more than just an adventurer; he is a Mountaineering expert, Professional Rock Climber, and Hiker rolled into one dynamic personality. With over 25 years of experience exploring the wilderness, Pranesh has honed his skills amidst the untamed landscapes and challenging terrains.

His journey as a mountaineer has taken him to the highest peaks and most remote regions, where he has conquered nature's grandeur with unwavering determination and unparalleled expertise. As a Professional Rock Climber, Pranesh has scaled vertical cliffs and rocky crags, mastering the art of vertical ascent with grace and precision.

However, Pranesh's passion extends beyond the sheer heights of mountains; he is also a seasoned Hiker, traversing vast trails and rugged landscapes with ease and finesse. His intimate connection with nature transcends mere exploration; it embodies a deep reverence and respect for the environment.

Pranesh's infectious enthusiasm and boundless energy inspire others to embark on transformative journeys, discovering the beauty of nature while pushing their own limits. His dedication to the trekking community serves as a beacon of inspiration, igniting the spirit of adventure in all who cross his path.

Passions and Achievements:
  • A professional mountaineer, rock climber, and hiking expert, Pranesh's versatility knows no bounds.
  • His journey into climbing was ignited during a state award ceremony, where he witnessed rock climbers in action.
  • Pranesh is a sought-after instructor with NOLS (National Outdoor Leadership School), traveling the globe to impart his expertise in mountaineering, rock climbing, and backpacking.
  • He's credited with pioneering new climbing routes in India, notably in Karnataka, and serves as a National route setter for climbing competitions.
