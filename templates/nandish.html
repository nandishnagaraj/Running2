Nandish Kumar - Fitness and Running Coach

I am not just a fitness enthusiast but a testament to determination and consistency. My journey began in 2011-12 when playing with my young daughter became a challenge. Weighing over 105 kilos, I decided to reclaim my college-level fitness.

The path wasn't easy. I started with swimming, then tried badminton and cycling. In 2013, a friend introduced me to a running group at Cubbon Park. Balancing family and fitness was tough, but weekend runs became a routine.

What began as a weekend activity soon became a passion. In 2015, I joined a running club closer to home. Here, I discovered the power of community. Running became a shared journey rather than a solo effort.

Over the years, I've completed many races, including 8 full marathons. My personal best is 3:39, achieved in 2022. For me, fitness is about habits, consistency, and the right environment. Small daily improvements lead to big progress.
